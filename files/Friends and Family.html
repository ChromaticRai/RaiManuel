Family and Friends

I've would never have gone this far without my family and friends that are always by my side no matter what.

My family has been the most supportive, understanding and patient of people that I've ever been with. With all the achievements I make and troubles that I've been through, they have been and always will be there no matter what and I can't be ever more grateful.

And about my friends? I couldn't feel ever less lonelier without them. In a cruel world where people can easily judge you or betray your trust, they're the ones who stayed true and I could've never asked for more. Their company for me is enough.
